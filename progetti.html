Gardening

Living in the Italian countryside, after London has given me a different relationship with nature. I love gardening and cultivate fruits that grew naturally here when we moved in. Because there were wild strawberries, I have planted several varieties of cultivated strawberries, the same goes for blackberries, and raspberries.

Beekeeping

The bees were a progression from growing fruit as they pollinate the plants and increase the yield. There is something very therapeutic about getting to know bees, it teaches you about how things work on a wider scale and how unimportant we humans really are.
